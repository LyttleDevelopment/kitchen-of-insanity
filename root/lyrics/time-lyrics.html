This mini CD was released after the debut album, Dreamaway Sunday.
Besides the single Time, and remixes of two further tracks from the first album,
the CD contains two more songs: Come Along and All The Things, two of the band's primal professional recordings.
Although both are great songs in their own right, they don't entirely meet the high standards of the album, admittedly in terms of lyrics.
