FIL YZERDRAAD, from the early 1980's to 2006!
From NACHT UND NEBEL to the Kitchen!



Most famous for their 1984 world hit record BEATS OF LOVE,
Nacht und Nebel was one of Belgium's most popular
bands at the time.



Fil joined the Kitchen in time to add Orpheus Feeling
to his curriculum vitae and stuck with the band
until after Loser's Manual.



In 2000 Fil briefly returned for Beg Your Pardon.
